Grade 11 - I met people with the vibes I really love and my circle of friends, we all got a title "with honor". I became fond of them as well as the teachers in Jesus Reigns Christian Academy. I get to experience retreat where we spend a night in Amadeo, Cavite. We worship God, Listen to pastors about how they given a chance by the God, and there, I really felt God, talking to me, to us students. It was the best feeling and experience.

Grade 12 - Pandemic begun. New normal. No more physical contact from friends. Online classes/Asynchronous classes only. No more gala after school.
